A Little About Me

My name is Kirsten Mercado, and I am a fourth-year student studying at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. After my co-op term as a Graphic Design Co-op at Precision NanoSystems (PNI), I have great interest in graphic design, particularly in print media and branding.

Working as a graphic designer is a rewarding experience because it makes me feel proud that my creative designs are displayed on various platforms for people to see. Majority of the graphic design projects I have worked on are created in Adobe Creative Suite, notably in Illustrator and InDesign.
